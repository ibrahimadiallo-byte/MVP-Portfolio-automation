Young Black Filmmakers Celebration - Harbor Picture Company x Reel Works

This project was a collaborative effort between Harbor Picture Company and Reel Works to celebrate and spotlight emerging Black filmmakers making waves in the industry. I served as both the lead editor and a camera operator for the event, capturing the energy and creativity of these talented artists as they showcased their work. The highlight reel brought together powerful moments from the evening, weaving together filmmaker interviews, film excerpts, and the genuine excitement of a community coming together to support the next generation of storytellers. It was an honor to not only craft the final edit but to be present at the event itself, documenting the passion and artistry that filled the room. This project reinforced my belief in the importance of amplifying diverse voices in film and celebrating the impact they have on reshaping the industry.

Skills Used:

Video Editing Adobe Premiere Motion Graphics Event Videography

The Veil - Official Trailer

During my internship at Reel Works, I was brought on as the editor for this short film's trailer. The goal was straightforward but critical: craft a trailer that immediately pulls viewers into the unsettling atmosphere of the story. I leaned heavily into horror aesthetics, using moody visuals and carefully selected sound design to build tension from the first frame. The color grading and pacing were designed to create that slow-burn dread that makes you want to keep watching, while the audio choices amplified the eerie, suspenseful tone. This project taught me a lot about how much power editing has in shaping audience perception and setting expectations for a film's genre and mood before they even hit play.

Skills Used:

Trailer Editing Storytelling Pacing & Timing Adobe Premiere Pro

Chris Poindexter Honoree Video - Reel Works 2024 Gala

This project stands out as one of my most challenging and rewarding edits during my time at Reel Works. I was tasked with creating an honoree video for board member Chris Poindexter, who was being recognized at the 2024 Reel Works Gala. The edit required weaving together interview footage with archival material, clips from Spike Lee films, and a mix of old and new Reel Works content to tell a story about the organization's evolution and Chris's impact on it. The pressure was on to deliver something polished and professional for such a significant event, and working closely with the team helped me navigate the complexity of balancing nostalgia with present-day achievements. This project pushed my skills in narrative structure, archival research, and crafting an emotionally resonant piece that honored both an individual and an organization's legacy.

Skills Used:

Archival Footage Integration After Effects Documentary Editing Adobe Premiere Pro
